Title (String): The name of the book.

Price (Float): The cost of the book. We assume it's in a single currency (e.g., USD).

Stock (String): Availability. As noted, every entry is "In stock".

Rating (from 5) (Integer): A numerical rating from 1 to 5.

Category (String): The genre or classification of the book.

Link (String): The URL to the book's page.
